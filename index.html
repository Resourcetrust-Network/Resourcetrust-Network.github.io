Resourcetrust Network (RTN) “knowledge and Practice”

A world of dignified human life, because business and government practices as well as consumer choices put human and the life of our planet at the centre.

We lead actions in sustainable natural resources management practices, focusing resource regeneration; implementation of best environmental and social practices in agriculture and forest productive systems and their supply chains for biodiversity conservation, livelihood and climate resilience.

The Challenge

How do we ensure a dignified human life without pushing our planet beyond its planetary boundaries?

How do we produce enough food to feed the projected Ten (10) billion people on the planet by 2050?

How do we produce the agricultural and forestry commodities we need without decimating our forests and other natural ecosystems and eroding the biodiversity of our planet? Imagine a world without insects?
