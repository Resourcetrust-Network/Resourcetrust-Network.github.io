Smallholder producers “a world of dignified human life?”

Most of the deforestation and other natural ecosystems conversion risk commodities (Cocoa, Oil Palm, Rubber, soy, cashew among others) are produced largely by smallholders living below the poverty line without adequate sustainable production capacities We lead working on the ground supporting smallholders and smallholder production communities to: implement best social and environmental management practices in their production systems and communities, diversify their income sources support their inclusion in ethical supply chains Our tested smallholder support vehicle is the Participate, Collaborate and Contribute (PCC) Approach and using contemporary tools (HCV-HCS) and technology. We actively participate in global sustainability initiatives, platforms and programmes to bring the smallholder production context to bear on policies, standards development and development decisions.
